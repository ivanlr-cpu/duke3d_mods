Description          This is a map of Christus scholl since 1993-1997, the
                        best school of Fortaleza-Ceara on Brazil. (updated
                        1999, 2003 and 2025, maintaining 1997 setup: 
			That school still exists buth real design changed)
                        
                        As I want to keep an endless past dimention, as
                        I could change this present and future (like with a
                        time machine), I've kept this golden design as a
                        'open timegate' for everyone to feel how this college
                        acted up. 

History               This school exists in Fortaleza before 1970. While enjoying
                        my life dimention between 90´s generations of people, I´ve
      			ready to create this map as a way to unleash another dimention
			between real life and another universe one of past and mystery. 
			It may sounds silly a first, but in short words: this is not a raw map 
			with no history and heart. It´s amazingly full of action, memories (and new ones)
			and it would be used as a base for ´dinamic day-night´ environments,
			as well as addons (ambiance sounds, tricks, new actor movements
			like teaching a matter in the blackboard and play soccer while
			in break time...)
